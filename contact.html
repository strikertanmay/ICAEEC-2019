PARTNERSHIP ASPECTS

The Indian group has a very strong hold in the area of Machine Learning. The Indian group has publication records in numerous ventures of repute related to machine learning. The expertise in machine learning extends to the applied areas of numerous vision, signal processing and other complex application areas. Specifically, the Indian PI has developed a large number of algorithms for distributed machine learning and machine learning for big data. This forms a backbone for the project. Further, the Indian group also has a strong understanding on feedback mechanisms, control mechanisms and using various modalities for feedback. The Indian Co-PI specifically works in robotics and brings to the project the experience in using robots as a means of delivering feedback to a human for an effective socialistic interaction

The Malaysian group has a state-of-the-art big data infrastructure and have extensive experience in using big data and high performance computing for problems of industrial utility. The group specializes in rigorous analysis of the data operated on the big data center. The group carries a lot of practical exposure to such systems.

The Indonesian side has an expertise in the textual aspect of the problem. A lot of knowledge for this domain is not in the form of raw sensory data, but in the form of common knowledge nicely documented at different sources. The Indonesian side has a vast experience in text mining such sources in quest of information.

So the expertise in the design of distributed machine learning algorithms of the Indian side, compliments the expertise in handling of high performance computing at the Malaysian side. Similarly the expertise in designing of interactive interfaces at the Indian side, compliments the ability to process this data on a big data framework and carry critical analysis at the Malaysian side. The ability to handle raw sensory data at the Indian and Malaysian side compliments the ability to mine information available as natural language at the Indonesian front.
